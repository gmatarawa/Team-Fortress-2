Meet the team

Team Fortress 2 features a roster of 9 different classes, each of which has their own set of weapons, playstyles, walking speeds, and health values. These are Scout, Soldier, Pyro, Demoman, Heavy Weapons Guy, Engineer, Medic, Sniper and Spy. Each of these classes has a different role to play, and some even have subclasses. While 9 classes may not seem like that many, it's made up for in the number of weapons available.
